1. Introduction

1.1 Overview

GreenThümb is a mobile application that allows users to approach the natural world around them as an opertunity to learn. People with this app can use their device's camera to photograph plants and allow our ML software to identify it. After identifying hte plant, they will be able to see information related to their plant. They can also browse our database of available plants to learn more about them.

1.2 Purpose

The purpose of this document is to establish the architexture and system design for the programmers to follow. This is important in assuring that all portions of the project are compatable accounting for the large number of members in the group. As long as each individual follows this plan, there should be minimal trouble with assembling the final project.

1.3 Scope

This document is for the developers of the project to ensure consistency and corectness in the implementation of the software.

1.4 Definitions
